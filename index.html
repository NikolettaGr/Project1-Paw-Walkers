About Us

Here when you need us

Whether you're looking for daily walks, planning a trip, stuck at work, or just want your best friend to have some company — we offer any day, anytime care.

Safety is serious business

Your dog's safety is our top priority. Every Pet Caregiver passes an enhanced background check, our services are insured, and support is standing by around the clock.

You can trust us

All sitters pass an identity verification check. All sitters provide a detailed profile and personal information. All sitters are approved by our team of sitter specialists.

Anytime support

Our Customer Support team is available to you and your pet 24/7.

Services

Dog Walking

Choose between a 20-minute, 30-minute, or 60-minute stroll for your dog. On-demand or pre-scheduled

House Sitting

Your sitter takes care of your pets and your home. Your pets will get all the attention they need without leaving home.

Boarding

Your pets stay overnight in your sitter’s home. They’ll be treated like part of the family in a comfortable environment.

Drop-in Visits

Your sitter drops by your home to play with your pets, offer food and 20-minute walky.Good option while you are at work.

Training

For dogs and cats of all ages and experience levels - work one-on-one with an experienced pet trainer from the comfort of your home, or over video chat.
